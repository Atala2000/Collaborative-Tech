Who are we

CTL is a technologies solutions provider focused on assisting clients acquire, implement and/or optimize the converging systems within their organization. CTL is a proudly Kenya based company delivering business convergent technology solution and risk protection services within the EAC and across Africa. CTL is comprised of senior business leaders well versed in technologies, methodologies and strategies capable of helping clients identify and overcome the product, processes, people, sales and risk challenges they may be facing.

Why Us

The changing digital enterprise landscape is bringing change and opportunity to all organizations wishing to leverage to their benefits of converging technologies. As enterprises look to move more workloads off-premises, they need strategies to mitigate risk and ensure that their service providers can work with any remaining on-premises environments, other key external service providers, while complying with governance and compliance requirements that are also changing. It would be impractical to think of conducting business without digital technologies. They form the backbone of the digital enterprise and also are key to aligning the business with IT for efficiency and agility. Solely placing emphasis on digital infrastructures and the alignment of business with IT are, however, insufficient for the future of the truly collaborative digital enterprise. There is much more at stake in the digital evolution. The digital enterprise needs to also address the additional convergence of applications, data and the capacities to execute, store and connect, truly the convergence of a portfolio of services and choices that offer an optimized combination of policy, placement and capabilities.
